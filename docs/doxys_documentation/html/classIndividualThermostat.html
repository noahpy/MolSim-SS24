double IndividualThermostat::getTotalKineticEnergy ( Simulation & sim)
overridevirtual

Calculates the total kinetic energy of the particles in the simulation while ignoring the mean velocity. This is used in problem sheet 5.

Attention
This is not the total kinetic energy of the system, but actually double of it. This is because the temperature calculations also divide by 2.
Parameters
simThe simulation to get the total kinetic energy for.
Returns
The total kinetic energy of the particles in the simulation (ignoring mean velocity).
