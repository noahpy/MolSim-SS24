Constructor to initialize a spherical particle clusters. 

A sphere is either a singe disc in the case of 2D spheres or a stack of discs with decreasing radii. Discs are a collection of rings. 
\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em origin} & The center of the sphere \\
\hline
{\em radius} & The radius of the sphere to be generated in number of particles (i.\+e. 1 -\/\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} a single particle, 2 -\/\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} single particle in the center surrounded by a single circle of particles, etc.) \\
\hline
{\em sphere\+Dimensions} & The dimensionality of the sphere (must be 2 i.\+e. disc or 3 i.\+e. sphere) \\
\hline
{\em spacing} & The spacing between the particles (i.\+e. separation between the discs and the distance along the circumference of a given ring) \\
\hline
{\em mass} & The mass of the particles within the cluster \\
\hline
{\em initial\+Velocity} & The initial velocity of all particles in the cluster \\
\hline
{\em mean\+Velocity} & The mean velocity of all particles in the cluster (passed to \doxylink{MaxwellBoltzmannDistribution_8h_a4072527de9b13fe5fb563ef877ae78c5}{maxwell\+Boltzmann\+Distributed\+Velocity()}) \\
\hline
{\em brownian\+Motion\+Dimensions} & The dimensions of the cluster (\texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}= 3) will be passed to \doxylink{MaxwellBoltzmannDistribution_8h_a4072527de9b13fe5fb563ef877ae78c5}{maxwell\+Boltzmann\+Distributed\+Velocity()} if set to 0 no brownian motion will be added, if set to 1 then only on the x axis, then x+y, and finally x+y+z axis \\
\hline
{\em stationary\+Particle\+Types} & A map of particle types that are stationary \\
\hline
{\em ptype} & The particle type for this cluster \\
